Timeline

What happens when a cohort fills.

1

Cohort reaches 150

Threshold met. Whollar verifies all addresses against the coverage map before proceeding.

2

Auction opens

Registered ISPs place sealed bids within 7 days. No bidder sees a competing offer.

3

Winner announced

Scored 60% on price, 25% on service commitment, 15% on activation speed.

4

You decide

Each household chooses to switch or stay. No obligation, no penalty either way.

Neighbourhood FAQ

150 verified households is the minimum cohort size. Below that, ISPs don't see enough density to justify the bid. 150 is the floor; cohorts can be up to 250.

WHL-ON-001 (Ottawa East) closes in 5 days. WHL-ON-002 (Orléans) and WHL-ON-003 (Barrhaven) are still forming — no close date until threshold.

Yes — one household, one cohort, based on your postal code. You can't join multiple cohorts.

If you join before the cohort hits threshold, you're in the auction. After the auction closes, the cohort is locked. New members join the next cohort for their FSA.
