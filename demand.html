Verification methodology

How we verify demand is real.

Every household in a Whollar cohort has completed all three steps before it counts toward threshold.

1

Address qualification

FSA lookup confirms the postal code resolves to a verified Ottawa address inside the cohort boundary. No mismatched postcodes — every household must resolve to a valid Ottawa address within the cohort’s FSA zone before it is counted.

2

Opt-in consent

Explicit PIPEDA / Québec Law 25 consent collected at sign-up. Households are given a clear data disclosure before their information is logged. Consent is stored and timestamped against each record.

3

Plan + contract capture

Current provider, approximate monthly bill ($), and contract end date captured at time of opt-in. This is the contract-end alignment that makes cohorts auction-ready — you bid on verified intent, not cold leads.
